**Example 1: 获取指定网关设备的子设备列表**



Input: 

```
tccli iotexplorer GetGatewaySubDeviceList --cli-unfold-argument  \
    --GatewayProductId productId \
    --GatewayDeviceName deviceName \
    --Offset 0 \
    --Limit 10
```
